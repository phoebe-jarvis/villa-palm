The Location

Kalkan — the Aegean coast's best-kept secret

Perched above a sparkling bay on Turkey's Turquoise Coast, Kalkan is a whitewashed hillside town known for its upmarket restaurants, boutique shopping and some of the clearest water in the Mediterranean. It's the ideal base for a group looking for culture, cuisine and complete relaxation.

🏖

Beaches & boat days

Kaputas Beach is 10 minutes away. Hire a boat for the day and explore hidden coves and mud baths.

🍽

Rooftop dining

Kalkan's rooftop restaurants are legendary. Seafood mezze, candlelit evenings, and sunsets over the bay.

🏛

Culture & day trips

Iincredible day trips for the whole group, including Patara ancient ruins, the sunken city of Kekova, Saklikent Gorge and Meis island.

✈️

Easy to reach

1.5 hours from Dalaman Airport (DLM). Flights from most UK airports throughout the summer season.
